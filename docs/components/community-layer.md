# Community Layer

The **engagement surface** of the exchange — where the people behind the data interact.

## Capabilities

- **Discussions** — question-and-answer threads attached to datasets and topics, so consumers can ask providers about data semantics, quality, and usage.
- **Challenges** — providers and platform operators publish data challenges; participants submit solutions built on exchange data.
- **Moderation & categories** — structured spaces with category-based organisation.

## Why it is part of the platform

A data exchange is only useful if consumers can understand the data they find. Keeping discussion alongside the catalogue — under the same identity and organisation model — means questions, answers, and challenge results stay connected to the resources they are about.

## Interactions

| Talks to | Why |
|---|---|
| Gateway / Identity | Same single sign-on as the rest of the platform |
| Control Plane (events) | Email notifications for submissions and replies |
